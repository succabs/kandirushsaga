Kandi Rush Saga

Instructions

The objective of the game is to get 180 student credits (opintopiste) before you run out of student allowance months (opintotukikuukausi).

Use left and right arrows to move, spacebar to shoot.

Numbers are student credits that you have to shoot to get them. The higher you shoot them, the better course number you get. That affects your total GPA (Keskiarvo).

Since beer is healthy, it gives you +1 student allowance months.

Selvityspyyntö has 5 health. Shoot it five times and it disappears. If it passes you or you get hit by it, you lose 25 student credits and 10 student allowance months.

You can't do anything about plagiointisyytös, but to pass it in the safe area somewhere inside it. If you get hit by it, you will lose the game.

Moodlen käyttökatko can hit you anytime. During that, you can't move or shoot for 4 seconds.

Oh, and if the game wasn't hard enough already, you lose one student allowance month every 10 seconds.

FAQ

Why is it cluttered with so many things?

Well, that happens in student life.

How am I supposed to cope with that?

You are not. Getting a degree is hard. Deal with it. But unlike in real life, this game lets you try again easily.

What if I get both selvityspyyntö and plagiointisyytös at the same time?

That is harsh. See the other answers.
